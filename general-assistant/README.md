# Gia — general assistant bundle

A capable, friendly all-rounder — the easiest first hire. Keeps the inbox and
calendar under control, captures notes and to-dos, looks things up, and drafts
whatever needs sending — always waiting for a yes before anything goes out. If
you're not sure which agent to start with, start here.

## How it works

- **Email** via the apps tool — triages the inbox and **drafts** replies through
  **Gmail or Outlook** (never sends).
- **Calendar** via **Google Calendar or Outlook** — reads the day/week, finds
  times (free/busy), holds tentatively, and books/moves only after approval.
- **Notes, to-dos & research** — captures to-dos + reminders (built-in tasks),
  keeps notes/docs in **Notion**, and answers "find out…" with **web-search**
  (short answer + source, not a link dump).

The three skills (`email-assistant`, `calendar-assistant`,
`notes-tasks-research`) are **knowledge** — real endpoints + the multi-provider
gotchas, kept approachable.

## Connect at /integrations
- **Gmail / Outlook** (required) — the inbox.
- **Google Calendar / Outlook** (required) — scheduling.
- **Notion** (optional) — notes + docs (built-in tasks work without it).
- **Slack** (optional channel) — get reminders/updates in Slack instead of email.

## Guardrails (baked in)
- **Nothing sends, books, buys, or commits without your yes** — Gia drafts,
  arranges, captures, and finds; you approve.
- **Nothing gets lost** — every to-do and promised follow-up is captured with a
  reminder.

Edit `knowledge/preferences.md` to tell Gia how you like things — tone, VIPs,
scheduling defaults, and what always needs your approval.
