# Ren — research analyst bundle

A rigorous research analyst. Takes a fuzzy question, digs across many sources,
vets what's credible, and comes back with a clear, **cited** answer — market
research, competitive analysis, due diligence, or a literature review. Every
claim has a source and a confidence level; nothing is stated as fact that isn't.

## How it works

- **Deep research** via **web-search** — decomposes the question into angles,
  runs multiple queries per angle, **triangulates** across independent sources,
  traces claims to primary sources, and hunts the counter-evidence.
- **Document research** via **Google Drive** + **Notion** — reads the company's
  own reports, decks, and knowledge base (exporting Google-native files) so
  research builds on what's already known.
- **Report writing** via **Notion** — an answer-first report: bottom line +
  confidence, findings with citations + confidence levels, disagreements, open
  questions, and a full source list. Delivers the headline + link via **Slack**.

The three skills (`deep-research`, `document-research`, `report-writing`) are
**knowledge** — the research *method* (source tiers, triangulation, confidence)
plus real Drive/Notion endpoints + gotchas (exporting Google files, Notion
report pages).

## Connect at /integrations
- **Notion** — the research library + knowledge base.
- **Google Drive** — internal source documents.
- **Slack** (optional channel) — deliver findings + report links.
- (Web research needs no connection — it's built in.)

## Guardrails (baked in)
- **Every claim has a source + a confidence level** — nothing uncited is stated
  as fact; nothing is fabricated.
- **Analysis is labeled** — a judgment call is never presented as settled truth.
- **Disagreements + uncertainty are surfaced**, never laundered into false
  certainty.

Edit `knowledge/research-standards.md` to set your source-credibility tiers,
confidence rubric, and citation format without touching the persona.
